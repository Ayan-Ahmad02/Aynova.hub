About Aynova

Aynova is your all-in-one platform offering entertainment, education, shopping, and lifestyle services. From the latest movies and music to university info, food delivery, and hostel bookings — Aynova brings everything you need under one roof.

Our mission is to provide a seamless, user-friendly experience with secure account management and personalized content tailored to your interests.

How to Use Aynova

  • Browse Content: Explore trending movies, music, university info, shopping deals, and more on the homepage.
  • Search: Use the search bar at the top to quickly find your favorite content or services.
  • Account Management: Create an account or sign in to personalize your experience, save favorites, and access premium features.
  • Subscription: Unlock full site access and premium content by subscribing via the subscription page.
  • Support: Visit the Help Me or Contact Us pages for assistance or to send us your feedback.

Account Login & Profile

To access your Aynova account:

  1. Go to the Account Login page.
  2. Enter your registered email and password to sign in.
  3. If you are new, create an account via the Registration page.
  4. Once logged in, you can update your profile, change your password, and upload a profile photo.
  5. Remember to log out after your session for security.
